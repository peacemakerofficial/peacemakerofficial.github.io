How does a VPN proxy work?

Product: How-To By: Markel Created: (27,05,2022 at 18:20 PM)

PIA Proxy Manager has more than 7800 high-speed proxy servers. When you connect a device to a proxy server, the device behaves as if it were on the same local network as the proxy server. Your device's internet connection is routed through a server of your choice. Once transmitted to the internet, your data will come from the proxy, not your device, and your real IP address will be hidden. It will create a secure tunnel where your data is encrypted from your device to the internet.


A VPN proxy allows you to securely access local web resources even if you are on the other side of the world. You're also free to surf the web as if you were at your current IP location, which has some benefits if you're on public Wi-Fi or want to access geo-blocked websites.
